Results

The best insulator is the material with the smallest temperature change and the slowest rate of cooling. In these results, bubble wrap performed best.

What ΔT Means

ΔT means change in temperature. In this project it is calculated as: starting temperature minus final temperature. For example, if water starts at 70.0°C and ends at 65.0°C, then ΔT = 5.0°C. A smaller ΔT means less heat was lost.
